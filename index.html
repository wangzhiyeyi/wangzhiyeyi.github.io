About Me

I am currently a Ph.D. student in Accounting at the Southwestern University of Finance and Economics. My research focuses on textual analysis and corporate disclosure strategies.

My work examines how firms communicate with stakeholders through financial reports and other disclosures, and how these communications affect market participants' decisions.
